## Protein Structure Prediction

### Objective

To predict the three-dimensional (3D) structure of a protein based on its amino acid sequence.

### Tools Used

* AlphaFold DB
* PyMOL 

### Method

* A protein sequence was obtained from UniProt.
* The sequence was submitted to a structure prediction tool, AlphaFold DB
* The predicted 3D structure was downloaded.
* Visualization software PyMOL was used to examine structural features.

### Results

* The predicted structure showed common protein features such as alpha-helices and beta-sheets.
* Structural folding patterns were observed, indicating potential functional domains.
* The model provided insight into the spatial arrangement of amino acids.
